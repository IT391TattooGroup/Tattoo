Ryein has been tattooing since 2002, and specializes in black and gray realism, portraits, and Japanese traditional work.

-He is at the shop from 12pm-8pm every day of the week except THURSDAYS and SUNDAYS

 Anthony has been tattooing since 2009, and has spent that time developing his style into a unique combination of traditional and illustrative work.

-He is at the shop from 12pm-8pm THURSDAY through MONDAY, except 12pm-5pm on SUNDAYS.

 Trevor has been tattooing since 2011. He enjoys tattooing everything from skulls, insects, and anatomical hearts, to flowers, script, and anything to do with outer space. Come see him at the shop and let him put a unique spin on your next piece.

-He is at the shop 12pm-8pm TUESDAY through SATURDAY.
